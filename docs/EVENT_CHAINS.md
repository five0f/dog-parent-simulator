# Event chains

Version: 1.0

## Overview

Event chains are long-term consequences of player actions.

They are not random. Every chain begins with player behavior.

The player creates the problem, solves the problem, and experiences the result.

## Chain 1: does not hold until walk

### Stage 1

Event: Bublik sits near the door.

Choices:

- Go for a walk
- Later
- Ignore

### Stage 2

Trigger: player ignores walk requests multiple times.

Event: Bublik brings the leash.

Choices:

- Go for a walk
- Later
- Ignore

### Stage 3

Trigger: player continues ignoring.

Event: Bublik becomes restless.

Description: he keeps walking between the door and the room.

Choices:

- Walk
- Wait
- Ignore

### Stage 4

Trigger: player ignores again.

Event: accident at home.

Description: Bublik could not wait.

Problem unlocked: does not hold until walk.

### Consequences

New events become available:

- Bublik asks to go outside more often
- Bublik becomes anxious near the door
- Bublik struggles to wait

### Recovery

Player consistently responds to walk requests and rewards successful outdoor toilet behavior.

After enough successful repetitions, the problem is removed.

Trait gained: responsible routine.

## Chain 2: pulls on leash

### Stage 1

Event: Bublik walks calmly.

Choices:

- Reward calm walking
- Ignore calm behavior
- Rush forward

### Stage 2

Trigger: player repeatedly rushes walks.

Event: Bublik begins pulling slightly.

### Stage 3

Trigger: player continues reinforcing pulling.

Event: Bublik drags toward interesting smells.

Problem unlocked: pulls on leash.

### Consequences

New events:

- Bublik rushes toward pigeons
- Bublik pulls toward dogs
- Walks become harder

### Recovery

Player rewards calm walking repeatedly and chooses training responses.

Problem removed.

Trait gained: good leash manners.

## Chain 7: sock thief

### Stage 1

Event: Bublik steals a sock.

Choices:

- Laugh
- Chase him
- Trade for a treat

### Stage 2

Trigger: player repeatedly turns socks into a game.

Event: Bublik starts collecting socks.

### Stage 3

Habit unlocked: sock thief.

### Consequences

Funny events become available:

- Missing socks
- Sock collections
- Sock investigations

This is a harmless habit. Not every chain must be negative.

## Design rule

Problems must emerge naturally.

The player should understand: "I created this."

And later: "I fixed this."

The game is about raising a dog, not managing statistics.
